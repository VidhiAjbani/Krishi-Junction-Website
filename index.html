What is Krishi Junction?

Krishi Junction is an application developed by a trusted NGO that connects farmers with agricultural technology solutions and resources.

How can Krishi Junction help farmers?

Krishi Junction provides farmers with access to various agricultural technologies, including information, tools, and services, to enhance their farming practices and improve productivity.

How can farmers get started with Krishi Junction?

Farmers can download the Krishi Junction application from the trusted NGO's website or official app stores, install it on their smartphones, and create an account to access its features.
